Local Wineries

  • Yonah Mountain Vineyards: A family-owned winery with beautiful views and excellent wines, offering tours and tastings.
  • The Cottage Vineyard & Winery: Known for its stunning location and a wide selection of wines, perfect for a relaxing afternoon.

  • CeNita Vineyards and Winery: Enjoy a picturesque setting and a delightful array of wines.

House Rules

To ensure a seamless experience for all guests, we kindly ask that you observe the following:

  • No Pets: Pets are not permitted on the premises.
  • No Smoking: Our property is smoke-free.
  • Occupancy: Please adhere to the maximum occupancy limit specified in your reservation.
  • Quiet Hours: Respect our neighbors by observing quiet hours from 10 PM to 8 AM.
  • Cleanliness: Kindly maintain cleanliness and leave the home in a tidy condition upon departure.

Before you depart, please complete the following tasks:

  • Dispose of trash in designated bins.
  • Load and start the dishwasher.
  • Strip beds and leave used linens in the laundry room.
  • Check all rooms for personal belongings.
  • Ensure windows and doors are closed and locked.
  • Turn off all lights.
  • Leave keys in the designated location.
  • Notify us upon departure.
